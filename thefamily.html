What is family?

As it signifies something different to everyone, the word "family" is one of the most loosely defined concepts in the English language. While one person's family may consist only of the relatives who share their home, another may view family to encompass both close and far relatives. Someone another, on the other hand, considers their favourite circle of friends or their pets to be family. Families vary greatly, yet they always operate on the same premise: shared love and commitment.
